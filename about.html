Our Story

From Our Family to Yours

We're a small local bakery in the heart of Cape Town, baking fresh, from-scratch Bread, cookies, and cakes daily. We believe in real ingredients, old-fashioned techniques, and the simple joy of a great cookie.

Founded in 2018 by Keabetswe, our bakery was built on a simple belief: that everyone deserves to experience the joy of a perfect, handcrafted treat made with honest, local ingredients.

But more than just a bakery, we are a gathering place. We know your order by heart, we celebrate your milestones with you, and we are deeply grateful to be a small part of your day.

Our Baking Philosophy
